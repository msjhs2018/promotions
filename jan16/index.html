Cream & Wings!

It's time for another fundraiser! This time, the Class of 2018 at MSJHS is partnering with the Class of 2018 at IHS to put on an exciting Buffalo Wild Wings and CREAM fundraiser!

Check out the links, and download the one for your respective school (IHS here, MSJ here)! It is super important that you print and bring the flyer packet along with you. Like, really, really important. So important that it's in bold.
